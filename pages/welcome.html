Client Portal Dashboard

We’ve streamlined the signup process for our valued customers. Upon signing up, you’ll receive a welcome email containing links to this page and your client portal.

Please note: A password is not required to access your client portal. Instructions on how to set a password will be provided later in this slideshow.

Here is the main dashboard of the client portal. After logging in, you should see something similar. In the dashboard section of your client portal, you’ll find important details including your statement details, personal information, a preview of your card/bank details, and the dates of upcoming service visits located at the bottom of the dashboard.

If you need further assistance, feel free to reply to our office number or call us at 610-674-9021.

Additionally, if you’d like to schedule a meeting with Cameron to learn more about the Client Portal, you can do so by clicking here

Client Portal Profile

Here is the Profile section of your Client Portal. Here, you can create a password (optional), add a new bank account or credit card, and view your payment method history.

Client Portal Invoices

Menu > Finances > Invoices

This is the invoices page. You can view each individual invoice by their status, invoice number, invoice date, total, and the total amount paid. To open an invoice, click on the icon located to the right. You can then view and print each invoice for personal use.

Please note: The invoice date is the date the invoice was sent, not the date the service was completed.

Client Portal Estimates

Menu > Finances > Estimates

Here is the estimates page. You can view each individual estimate by its status, estimate number, estimate date, and amount. Click on the icon to the right to open and view or print each estimate for personal use.

For new customers, scheduling service requires a card on file. Once an estimate is accepted in your client portal, your service will be scheduled accordingly.

Client Portal Payments

Menu > Finances > Payments

Here is the payments page. You can view updates to your account, including details about new payments, credits added or removed, and more.

Please note that you cannot download payment receipts for personal use. If you require a printed receipt, please contact us for assistance.

Client Portal Work Requests

Here is the work requests page. You can submit a new work request to receive an estimate for one of our services. For direct support, please refrain from using the work request form and contact our office directly or call us at 610-674-9021.
